Apa Itu Teks Argumentatif? / What is an Argumentative Text?

(ID) Pernahkah kamu mencoba meyakinkan temanmu kalau tim sepak bola favoritmu adalah yang terbaik? Kamu pasti memberikan banyak alasan, kan? Nah, itulah inti dari teks argumentatif! Tujuannya adalah untuk **membujuk (persuade)** pembaca agar setuju dengan pendapatmu.
(EN) Have you ever tried to convince your friend that your favorite football team is the best? You must have given many reasons, right? Well, that's the core of an argumentative text! Its purpose is to **persuade** the reader to agree with your opinion.

Struktur Teks (Dengan Contoh) / Text Structure (With Examples) üèõÔ∏è

(ID) Bayangkan kamu adalah seorang pengacara. Ini bagian-bagian "kasus" yang harus kamu bangun:

(EN) Imagine you are a lawyer. These are the parts of the "case" you need to build:

1. Thesis (Your Main Idea / Ide Utama Kamu)

(ID) Ini adalah paragraf pembuka yang menyatakan pendapat utamamu.
(EN) This is the opening paragraph that states your main opinion.

Contoh / Example:

"Many students feel that homework should be reduced. I believe this is true because too much homework can cause stress and takes away time for other important activities."

2. Arguments (Your Reasons & Evidence / Alasan & Bukti Kamu)

(ID) Ini adalah inti dari tulisanmu. Berikan beberapa alasan kuat yang didukung oleh contoh untuk meyakinkan pembaca.
(EN) This is the core of your text. Give several strong reasons supported by examples to convince the reader.

Contoh / Example:

"First, a lot of homework often leads to stress and burnout. Students feel tired and cannot focus in class the next day. It reduces their time for family, hobbies, and rest."

3. Conclusion (The Final Statement / Kesimpulan Akhir)

(ID) Ini adalah paragraf penutup. Ulangi lagi pendapat utamamu dan rangkum alasan-alasan terkuatmu.
(EN) This is the closing paragraph. Restate your main opinion and summarize your strongest reasons.

Contoh / Example:

"In conclusion, by giving less homework, schools can help students to be healthier and happier. It is important to find a good balance between study and personal life."

(ID) Mari kita bedah teks sederhana tentang E-Money ini dan temukan strukturnya.

(EN) Let's break down this simple text about E-Money and find its structure.

Paragraf 1

Today, many people use e-money like GoPay, OVO, or ShopeePay. We can buy food, pay for transport, and shop online easily. But, some people still like to use cash. So, is e-money really better than cash? To answer this, we should look at the good sides and the bad sides of using e-money.

(ID) Menurutmu, paragraf ini bagian apa?
(EN) In your opinion, what part of the text is this?

Jawaban: Thesis
(ID) Karena paragraf ini memperkenalkan topik (e-money vs cash) dan menyatakan apa yang akan dibahas (sisi baik dan buruk).
(EN) Because this paragraph introduces the topic (e-money vs cash) and states what will be discussed (the good and bad sides).

Paragraf 2

The main reason people like e-money is because it is very convenient. First, you do not need to carry a lot of cash in your wallet. You only need your phone. Second, transactions are very fast. You just scan a QR code and the payment is done. You also don't need to worry about having the exact amount of money or waiting for change.

(ID) Kalau paragraf ini bagian apa?
(EN) What about this paragraph?

Klik di sini untuk lihat jawaban / Click here to see the answer
Jawaban: Argument (Argument For / Pro)
(ID) Karena paragraf ini memberikan alasan-alasan mengapa e-money itu baik (praktis, cepat, tidak perlu uang pas).
(EN) Because this paragraph gives reasons why e-money is good (convenient, fast, no need for exact change).

Paragraf 3

However, e-money also has some problems. First, you always need an internet connection and your phone must have battery. If your phone dies or there is no signal, you cannot pay. Second, there is a risk from hackers. Bad people can try to steal the money from your e-wallet balance. Finally, some people feel they spend more money with e-money because they don't see the physical cash leaving their hands.

(ID) Paragraf ketiga ini bagian apa?
(EN) What part is this third paragraph?

Klik di sini untuk lihat jawaban / Click here to see the answer
Jawaban: Argument (Argument Against / Kontra)
(ID) Karena paragraf ini memberikan alasan-alasan mengapa e-money itu bermasalah (butuh internet/baterai, risiko hacker).
(EN) Because this paragraph gives reasons why e-money is problematic (needs internet/battery, risk of hackers).

Paragraf 4

In conclusion, e-money has both advantages and disadvantages. It is fast and easy, but it also has risks like needing the internet and the danger of hackers. Therefore, it is important for us to be smart when using e-money. We should use it for its convenience, but we must always be careful to keep our accounts safe.

(ID) Karena paragraf ini merangkum argumen (cepat dan mudah TAPI berisiko) dan memberikan saran di akhir.
(EN) Because this paragraph summarizes the arguments (fast and easy BUT risky) and gives a recommendation at the end.
Ciri-Ciri Bahasa (Biar Makin Keren!) / Language Features (To Make it Cooler!) ‚ú®
  • Simple Present Tense: Menggunakan kata kerja bentuk sekarang karena membahas fakta. (e.g., *E-money **is** easy.*)
  • Persuasive Words (Kata-kata untuk Meyakinkan): Menggunakan kata-kata seperti *should* (sebaiknya), *must* (harus), *important* (penting), *better* (lebih baik).
  • Logical Connectives (Kata Penghubung Logis): Menggunakan kata seperti *First* (Pertama), *Second* (Kedua), *However* (Namun), *Therefore* (Oleh karena itu).
